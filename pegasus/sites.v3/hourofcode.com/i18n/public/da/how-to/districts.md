# Sådan får du Hour of Code i hele din kommune (skoledistrikt - USA).

## Få alle skoler til at deltage, alle elever til at lære!

  1. **Rekrutter lokale skoler.** Del [denne email](<%= resolve_url('/promote/resources#sample-emails') %>) og [lærervejledning](<%= resolve_url('/how-to') %>), eller inkluder en [kort reklametekst](<%= resolve_url('/promote/stats') %>) i nyhedsbreve/kommunale kommunikationsmidler.

  2. **Provide a sample logistics plan for schools.** Share this [sample logistics plan](<%= localized_file('/files/HOC_Logistics_plan.pdf') %>) with schools to give them ideas of how to organize whole school participation. Det er let at gøre det i matematiktimerne, eller skift mellem klasserne, så alle kan bruge computerrummet. De kan indsende deres egen plan for at have chancen for at vinde hardware for $10.000 på [http://hourofcode.com/prizes](<%= resolve_url('/prizes/hardware-signup') %>).

  3. **Del på de sociale medier.** Post på [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/sharer/sharer.php?u=http%3A%2F%2Fhourofcode.com%2Fus) eller [Twitter](https://twitter.com/intent/tweet?url=http%3A%2F%2Fhourofcode.com&text=I%27m%20participating%20in%20this%20year%27s%20%23HourOfCode%2C%20are%20you%3F%20%40codeorg&original_referer=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.google.com%2Furl%3Fq%3Dhttps%253A%252F%252Ftwitter.com%252Fshare%253Fhashtags%253D%2526amp%253Brelated%253Dcodeorg%2526amp%253Btext%253DI%252527m%252Bparticipating%252Bin%252Bthis%252Byear%252527s%252B%252523HourOfCode%25252C%252Bare%252Byou%25253F%252B%252540codeorg%2526amp%253Burl%253Dhttp%25253A%25252F%25252Fhourofcode.com%26sa%3DD%26sntz%3D1%26usg%3DAFQjCNE1GLTUbKZfMlEh9Aj5w0iswz6PYQ&related=codeorg&hashtags=). Eller del et af disse inspirerende [billeder og citater](<%= resolve_url('/promote/resources#social') %>) fra verdens førende navne, eller [statistik](<%= resolve_url('/promote/stats') %>).

  4. **Promote on your district’s homepage.** Let visitors know about your participation and Hour of Code events. Link it to [Hour of Code](<%= resolve_url('/') %>).

  5. **Vær vært for et skoledistrikts Hour of Code-arrangement.** Se vores [arrangementvejledning](<%= resolve_url('/how-to/events') %>) for et showeksempel, mediemateriale eller andet støttemateriale.

  6. **More [prizes](<%= resolve_url('/prizes') %>) for EVERY organizer.** Every participating educator will receive thank you gifts, while supplies last. Og i hver stat i USA, vil én deltagende skole vinde for $10.000 it-udstyr til deres skole!

## Hvad er næste skridt efter Hour of Code?

Hour of Code er blot det første skridt på en lang rejse for at lære mere om, hvordan teknologi fungerer, og hvordan du kan lave dine egne programmer. To continue this journey: - The Hour of Code is just the first step on a journey to learn more about how technology works and how to create software applications. To continue this journey:

  * Encourage students to continue to [learn online](<%= resolve_url('https://code.org/learn/beyond') %>).
  * [Attend](<%= resolve_url('https://code.org/professional-development-workshops') %>) a 1-day, in-person workshop to receive instruction from an experienced computer science facilitator. (US educators only)
